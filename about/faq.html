ABOUT ME

What is AR-3 ?

AR-3 stand for A Ray Ryan R, my pen name to be used and known over internet. I use AR-3 as my personal trademark, however sometimes it is also stand for a team that working under my wing. My real name is Ryan Aditya P. W., as visual artist, my artwork ranged from corporate identity, graphic design, interface, photography, icon, illustration, etc. I do these as part of my hobby and freelance, most of the time my artwork can't be published officialy because I work as ghost designer. I believe visual art can be used to express feeling, while visual design can be used to manipulate other.

Are you experienced ?

I start this all since my highschool, with Adobe Photoshop CS, in 2005 for student council with the trial and error of my self taught. Then I become a part of Indonesia art institute, ISI Yogyakarta, in 2008 to expand my knowledge about visual art. There, I learn art theory and other graphic related software. In 2009 local clothing company hire my as a ghost designer & photographer for their product until 2012. Since then, I become hiatus as a designer, but still somewhile I do freelance and helping local community or small company to grow with graphic design. Most of the artwork and design that I already made is related to the hobby, while other is for commercial purpose.

What softwares do you use?

In the box of my specialty is including Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustration & Adobe Indesign. I also use other Adobe software, Corel Draw, Google Sketchup, & Manga Studio. These software is to be used integrated since they have their own benefical. Out of the box as a support or more specific purpose, I also use software from microsoft, stardock, audacity, axialis, etc. Sometimes I also use software from not widely known company such as a shedko from Russia, or even use non direct design related software such notepat++ and hex editor which more familiar to the web coder/programmer rather than designer.

What style your art is?

Probably simple & clean. In my highschool I'm expressing my self with graffiti, clipping and I like colorfull art. However after I learn art theory, especially for graphic design, I'm more focused to the flow of visual reading, readibility/clarity, point of interest and focus, thus make my art more simple than ever. Especially, simplicity can bring integrity and better performance for multipurpose in this digital/information era. I'm also influenced by modern/profesional look in flat design, with traditional idealism still in the mind, such as symbolic meaning and detail.

ADDITIONAL INFORMATION ABOUT ART & DESIGN

Why design taking time?

Think about design like cooking. Cooking need proper ingredients, procedure, and correct time to cook. Information is the ingredients and different design need different ingridients. Procedure for cooking is basic in design, let say skip some procedure, it will look and taste different. Correct time to cook like brainstorming, too hasty may left better idea slip. Skip any of them, you'll get template like design which isn't special made and not healthy for you, just like junk food.

What if I can get cheaper service than you?

You can go to other designer if you think they're better suit you. Just like a food, junk food is cheap because they're to be mass produced (template like design). In logo for example there's bunch of template like design, you can even google it with easy. This case normally happened in free design, super cheap design, and especially design contest. I'm sure, people with same principle which aim quality design and taking time to brainstorming and carefully process will not giving them self to be paid cheaper than me, more professional they're will be more expensive they put their service price, because they stake their reputation on it.
