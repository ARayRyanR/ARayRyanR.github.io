// Logo, Corporate Identity & Branding Strategy.

AGA
Aquatic Gardeners Association
Texas, USA

The pictogram show magascar lace plant as organization long history tradition inside 3 bubble which symbolize H2O (Water) to associate organization with aquascape. While wordmark is chained together to simbolizing unity.

AR-3
AR-3 Visual Art
Bali, IDN

Stylized wordmark of AR-3. It has no sharp edge to emphasis modern design & human touch. Using gestalt theory there is 2 head animal inside it which represent balance between aesthetic and function.

DAZZLE
Danzer Zleman Community
Yogyakarta, IDN

Logo that represent a community of Danz Base game from Yogyakarta. Figure inside 'ZZ' obviously could show comunity focus, a dance. The magenta color is called 'razzle dazzle' that able to pinpoint as color identity.

LABA
LABA
Yogyakarta, IDN

Logo of LABA is designed for an auction, selling & buying group. It combine spider web and profit diagram as icon, both spider and profit can be translated as laba in Bahasa Indonesia. By combining these 2 element, logo represent mission of the group to gather people who are looking for trapping profit.

LIMO
LimO
For Sale

Limo is Javanese language for lime. In Java lime is most popular fruit to be used for culinary especially drink. The 'O' of the wordmark could represent company as stand alone icon and to be focus point in the logo. Typography used feel fresh and can be easy related with food & drink.

MONALIZA ABIDIN
Monaliza Abidin
Kediri, IDN

Personal identity for Monaliza, jewellery and fashion designer. The pictogram and wordmark used define feminim and strong psychological of a woman. Thus to symbolize it, diamond is to be used since it is most strong, beautiful and desired material on earth. White, black and violet-indigo color combined to look elegant yet left misterious feeling when looking at it, so it can be alluring.

OCTONES
Octones Clothing
Pekalongan, IDN

Mix between alien, octopus and musical note. Octopus symbolize company philosophy to be able to reach every narrow gap of oportunity. Musical note is a rhyme that symbolize harmony of quality and price of the product. While alien looking octopus head in the logo is made to be extremly catchy and able to make logo versatile in many media.

PANDORA
Pandora Team
Yogyakarta, IDN

Pandora is a short term publishing team that release e-book magazine about graphic design & visual art for ISI Yogyakarta. While the underline in the wordmark represent team unity the box that replace 'O' visually represent pandora box. Pandora box is choosed as a team name to simbolizing 'unknown content' inside magazine.

PHOENIX
Phoenix
For Sale

Phoenix is a mythical bird of rebirth that symbolize power and eternity. The logo is to be used for corporate that want longevity as a philosophy, so no matter how much the company get in trouble it will stand and rebirth. The 'O' character replaced by a bird that looking up to the sky, soaring ascend flying to symbolize hope and strong will.

THE FRILLED DRAGON
The Frilled Dragon Forum
New Jersey, USA

Inspired by popular frilled dragon photograph by National Geographic magazine. The logo show particular feature that only frilled dragon has, the frilled neck. Using 2 main color, black and orange which is color of the adult frilled dragon it self, it rebranding previous white and blue colored forum style to be easy associated with frilled dragon.

THE SONGKETS
The Songkets
Kediri, IDN

The songkets is company that selling hand made fashion wear from songket fabric. The 'K' character stylized to make logo look like fabric/flag material while 'O' character is replaced with songket pattern. Thus making logo represent what is it about.

ZERAPHALANX
Zeraphalanx
Chicago, USA

Personal identity for Kevin Smith. The concept come from personification of time (sand clock) & speed (arrow) as a symbol of the pictogram 'Z' which can be used to form wordmark Zeraphalanx. If rotated 45 degree clockwise, it will form balanced version that resemble crux as a faith symbol.
